Sahil Goyal : An Introduction

Sahil Goyal is a dedicated and senior leader associated with the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP), known for his strong commitment to nationalism, cultural values, and youth empowerment. He has consistently worked at the grassroots level to promote social harmony, national integrity, and ideological awareness. He currently serves as the National Minister of Bajrang Sena Bharat, where he actively contributes to initiatives focused on national interest and the preservation of Sanatan culture. In addition, Sahil Goyal holds the position of National President of Hindu Yuva Morcha, playing a key role in inspiring and organizing youth across the country towards nation-building and social responsibility.
